Journal of Culture & Retail Image

About the Journal

The Journal of Culture and Retail Image is a peer-reivewed publication of Drexel University's Design & Merchandising Department and Drexel University Libraries. The journal strives to expand both our students' research and writing skills as well as the larger field of retail research.

Focusing on the fashion retail environment and contemporary culture, each issue is dedicated to a particular topic and includes student papers from Drexel University students.

The first issue was electronically published on the world wide web in November 2008. Since then, the journal has grown and had a readership that expands the globe. It continues to be a source of research for scholars and helps others to develop their scholarship in the area of fashion and retailing studies.
